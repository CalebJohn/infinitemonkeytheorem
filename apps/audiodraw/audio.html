Instructions:

  • Just click and drag on the canvas to draw a line!
  • There are 2 input lines (both are necessary to produce sound):
  • The Volume line (black), this controls volume with respect to time
  • The frequency line (red), similarly this controls frequency with time
  • Use the "Toggle Line" button to switch between these
  • The slider to the right of the buttons controls adjusts the zoom level allowing you to input a longer or shorter tone
  • Currently the vertical lines represent 200ms time divisions
